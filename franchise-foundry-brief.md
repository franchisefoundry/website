*For use as context at the start of any Claude conversation*

## Who We Are

Franchise Foundry is a UK-based franchise matchmaking service founded by Harry and Ben — two hospitality operators who have built, scaled and recruited for real franchise brands. We are not brokers or portal managers. We came up through hospitality operations and have sat at the franchisor's recruitment table. That inside knowledge is now entirely on the franchisee's side.

**Email:** connect@franchisefoundry.co.uk

---

## The Business Model

Franchise Foundry operates a dual-sided matchmaking model:

**For franchisees (consumers):** The service is completely free. No consultation fees, no introduction fees, no hidden costs. Ever. We are paid by franchise brands when the right match is made — which aligns our incentives entirely with getting it right, not just getting it done.

**For franchise brands:** We work with a selective number of hospitality franchise brands at any one time. We assess the brand first, believe in the model, and only then bring it to our candidate network. Brands pay us when we successfully introduce the right franchisee. We are selective — we turn down brands we don't believe in.

**Revenue flow:** Brands → pay Franchise Foundry → when a franchisee is signed. Franchisees pay nothing.

---

## What Makes Us Different

The UK franchise brokerage market is built to move volume. Brokers are paid per signing. Portals are paid per click. Neither has a real incentive to find the right fit.

Franchise Foundry is the only franchisee-first matching service in the UK. The core differentiator is insider knowledge: we've sat at franchise recruitment tables, we know what franchisors actually look for, what makes a site work, where there is room to negotiate, and where the red flags are. We share all of that with the franchisee — before they ever sit down with a brand.

---

## How the Matching Process Works

1. **Franchisee tells us what they're looking for** — not just budget and postcode, but how they want to spend their days, what success looks like in year three, and what they won't compromise on.
2. **Our matching engine scores every brand in our network** against that profile: budget, location, lifestyle, ambitions, operating model. Objectively, without commission bias.
3. **We walk them through their matches properly** — unit economics, the model, the pressure points, the real picture. No rose-tinted pitch decks.
4. **We stay with them through to signing** — introductions, what to ask, what to push back on, what the franchisor is really looking for.

## Our Values

**Franchisee-first, always.** Every decision starts with what is right for the franchisee. We turn down brands we don't believe in. We won't match anyone with something we wouldn't back ourselves.

**Inside knowledge, openly shared.** We know what franchisors are looking for, where they flex, and where they won't. We give franchisees all of it before they walk in.

**Free. No small print.** Our service to franchisees is free. Always. No fees, no commission from the franchisee's side. We get paid by brands when we find them the right person — which means our interests align with theirs.

**Selective about brands.** We work with a limited number of brands at once. If we partner with a brand, we've assessed the model and we believe in it. That selectivity is what makes our introductions worth having.

**Reputation over volume.** Our reputation depends on getting it right, not just getting it done. If we put someone in front of the wrong brand, we've failed at the only thing that matters.
