Legal

Terms of Engagement

Please read these terms carefully before submitting your details. They set out how we work together and the protections in place for you and the brands we introduce you to.

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Franchise Foundry Ltd  ·  connect@franchisefoundry.co.uk

The short version — plain English

What you're agreeing to, in a nutshell

  • Our service is free to you. We're paid by the franchise brand if and when you sign a franchise agreement — not before, and never by you.
  • We match you based on fit, not fees. We're not tied to any single brand — your matches are based entirely on your profile and goals.
  • Your data stays private. We'll never sell your details or share them with anyone except the specific brands we introduce you to — and we'll tell you before we do.
  • One thing we ask: if we introduce you to a brand, please keep us involved. Don't approach that brand directly — that's how we keep the service free for you. This applies for 24 months from the date of any introduction.

The full legal terms are set out below. By ticking the box on our form, you're agreeing to both.

1. Who We Are

Franchise Foundry is a franchise brokerage and matching service operating in the UK hospitality sector. We act as an intermediary, connecting prospective franchisees with franchise brands that suit their goals, budget, and background.

Our service to you is provided free of charge. Franchise Foundry receives an introductory fee from the franchise brand if and when a successful franchise agreement is completed. This does not affect the impartiality of our matching process — we are not tied to any single brand and our recommendations are based solely on your profile.

By submitting your details through our website, you confirm that you have read and agree to these Terms of Engagement in full.

2. Our Role

Franchise Foundry will:

  • Use the information you provide to match you with suitable franchise opportunities from our network
  • Arrange and facilitate introductions between you and franchise brands on your behalf
  • Provide guidance and support throughout your enquiry process, free of charge
  • Act in your best interests when recommending matches

We do not provide legal, financial, or investment advice. We strongly recommend that you take independent professional advice before entering into any franchise agreement.

3. Non-Circumvention — Your Key Obligation

This is the most important clause in these terms, and we ask you to take it seriously.

When Franchise Foundry introduces you to a franchise brand — whether by sharing a brand name, arranging a meeting, or facilitating any communication — that introduction is made in our capacity as a broker. You agree not to approach, contact, or enter into any discussions or negotiations with that brand directly, or through any third party, without Franchise Foundry's knowledge and involvement.

This obligation applies:

  • From the date of the first introduction to that brand
  • For a period of 24 months from the date of that introduction
  • Whether or not you proceed with Franchise Foundry's assistance
  • Regardless of how the introduction was made (email, call, meeting, or otherwise)

If you breach this obligation, Franchise Foundry reserves the right to seek recovery of its introductory fee directly from you, equivalent to the fee that would have been payable by the franchise brand upon completion of a franchise agreement.

In plain terms: if we introduce you to a brand, please keep us in the loop. Going directly to a brand we've matched you with cuts us out of a process we've invested time and resource in — and it's something we take very seriously.

4. Your Information

By submitting the enquiry form, you consent to Franchise Foundry holding and processing your personal data for the purpose of providing our matching and introductory services. Specifically, we will:

  • Use your contact details (name, email, phone number) to get in touch with you about your matches
  • Share relevant profile information (budget range, location, preferences) with franchise brands we introduce you to — we will always tell you before doing so
  • Store your data securely for the duration of our working relationship

We will never sell your data or share it with any third party other than the specific franchise brands you are introduced to through our service.

You have the right to request that your data be amended or deleted at any time. To do so, contact us at connect@franchisefoundry.co.uk.

Our handling of your data is governed by UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

5. No Guarantee of Outcome

Franchise Foundry makes no guarantee that your enquiry will result in a franchise agreement, a meeting with a brand, or any particular outcome. Our matching service is provided in good faith, based on the information you supply. The ultimate decision to proceed with any franchise opportunity rests with you and the franchise brand concerned.

6. Accuracy of Information

You agree to provide accurate and truthful information when using our service. Providing false or misleading information — for example about your available budget or location — may result in poor-quality matches and wastes time for all parties involved.

7. Changes to These Terms

Franchise Foundry may update these terms from time to time. The current version will always be available at franchisefoundry.co.uk/terms.html. Your continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of the current terms.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising from these terms shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
