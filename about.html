Our Story

Franchise Foundry was founded by two franchise operators with over 30 years of combined experience building, scaling, and running successful franchise businesses across the UK.

We're not recruiters or brokers-we're people who've lived the franchisee journey firsthand. We've experienced the excitement of opening new locations, the challenges of maintaining brand standards, and the satisfaction of building something that lasts.

Now, we're using that hard-earned knowledge to help aspiring franchisees like you make better, more informed decisions about which franchise is right for you.

Why We Do This

Through our years in franchising, we've seen what happens when people rush into the wrong franchise. We've seen mismatches between franchisees and brands. We've watched good people struggle because they weren't properly prepared or didn't truly understand what they were getting into.

"Not every franchisee is the right franchisee-and not every franchise is the right fit for every person."

That's why we created Franchise Foundry. We wanted to change the way people find franchises-to make it more personal, more honest, and more focused on long-term success rather than quick sales.

Our Approach is Different

We don't just throw a list of franchises at you and hope something sticks. We take the time to understand you-your goals, your budget, your lifestyle, and what you're truly looking for in a business.

Then, we use our decades of experience to match you with opportunities that genuinely fit. And we're with you every step of the way, offering honest advice and ongoing support-completely free.
