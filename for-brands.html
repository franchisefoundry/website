Our matching engine surfaces candidates who fit your brand on data - investment, operating model, ambitions, background. Then our team takes over: honest conversations, proper prep, and introductions we actually believe in.

What sets our introductions apart.

Our matching platform does the objective scoring. Our team does the rest - proper candidate prep, honest conversations, and introductions we genuinely believe in.

Matched by data, confirmed by us

Our matching platform scores every candidate against your brand profile across budget, operating model, growth ambitions and background before any human review takes place. The people we bring forward have been assessed as right for your model by data first, then by us.

Prep that protects your time

We walk candidates through your concept and unit economics before they ever meet you. They arrive informed, with real questions - not a blank look at your franchise disclosure document.

Inside knowledge on both sides

We've sat at franchise recruitment tables. We know what makes a franchisee go the distance - and we share that honestly with everyone we work with.

Selective about who we work with

We work with a limited number of brands at once. If we partner with you, we've assessed your model and we believe in what you're offering. That selectivity shows in the quality of introductions.

Aligned incentives

We're paid by brands when the right match is made - which means we only make introductions we believe in. Our reputation is on the line with every one.

Involved through the process

We stay involved from first conversation to franchise award. If something's not working, you'll hear it from us before it becomes a problem.

How it works

From first conversation to franchise award.

1

Tell us about your ideal franchisee

Not just the checklist. We want to understand the profile behind the criteria - what makes someone succeed in your model, and what the common wrong-fits look like. That detail feeds directly into how our platform weights your matches.

2

Our platform does the matching

Your brand profile is run through our matching engine against every candidate in our network - investment level, operational model, growth ambitions, background. The platform scores fit objectively, with no bias toward availability or volume. We review the results before making any move.

3

We prep before we introduce

Before any introduction, we walk candidates through your concept, your unit economics, and what you're really looking for. They arrive prepared. So do you.

4

We stay involved

From first meeting to franchise award, we're in it with you. We manage expectations on both sides and help get the right people across the line.

Who we work with

We're selective about the brands we partner with. Here's what matters to us.

  • Hospitality franchise brands with clear and realistic unit economics
  • Established franchisee support systems and a genuine commitment to their success
  • Territory availability that makes introductions worthwhile
  • Transparency about investment requirements and what the model actually demands day to day
  • A long-term view on recruitment - the right people over fast numbers
